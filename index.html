Premier Distribution in Navsari

Shah Sales is a specialized distribution agency based in Navsari, Gujarat. We take goods directly from manufacturers and distribute them across a wide network of retailers throughout the Navsari district.

Our focus is exclusively on the Navsari market, allowing us to offer deep penetration and personalized service to every taluka, from the city center to the interior regions of Bansda and Chikhli.

Our Core Business Model

We act as the official logistics and sales arm for brands looking to establish a strong presence in Navsari. We manage warehousing, secondary sales, and doorstep retail delivery.
